Common Refrigerator Problems We Fix:

  1. ◈A faulty thermostat, which makes it hard for you to adjust the temperature
  2. ◈A defective start capacitor, which prevents the refrigerator from starting
  3. ◈Refilling the coolants if the levels are low
  4. ◈Cleaning of the dirty condenser coils to improve efficiency
  5. ◈Repairing of the faulty compressor (the compressor performs the refrigeration cycle to keep your refrigerator within a set temperature)
  6. ◈Insufficient voltage even when the home circuit is working right
  7. ◈A faulty motor that prevents the refrigerator from starting or causes it to shut down immediately
  8. ◈The ice cube maker fails to function or cool the water sufficiently
  9. ◈A fridge that is not cooling food sufficiently
  10. ◈Loud noises come from the refrigerator as it works
  11. ◈The motor gets hot over time
  12. ◈There is ice build-up in your freezer
